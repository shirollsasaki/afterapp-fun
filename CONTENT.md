## Section 5: COUNTER — "Why Apps Won't Vanish Overnight"

**Information vs. Action Gap:**
- AI can suggest a budget, cannot move money between accounts
- AI can recommend a restaurant, cannot process reservation through POS
- Cannot interact with banking APIs, deliver physical goods, read hardware sensors

**Hallucination:**
- 8-20% hallucination rates in clinical decision support
- GPT-4: 28.6% fabricated citations (Stanford)
- Finance: 27% of earnings predictions beyond 2 quarters = fabricated
- Hospital CEO turned off AI sepsis detection: "wrong 50% of the time"
- Consumers find AI errors LESS forgivable than human mistakes

**UX Reality:**
- Nielsen Norman Group: "Graphical interfaces support exploration, conversational interfaces support completion"
- Most app usage = browsing, comparing, visualizing (GUI strengths)
- Rabbit R1 market failure = voice-dependent devices can't replace touch
- TELUS survey: 58% reported NO change in app usage, 24% INCREASED app usage

**Business Model:**
- Only ~15% of AI companies have successful monetization
- Each AI query = compute cost that scales linearly (vs apps' near-zero marginal cost)
- Apple/Google not passively ceding — recreating App Store model for AI

**Gartner's Own Data (contradicts headline):**
- Only 12% of IT leaders strongly agree AI agents replace apps in 2-4 years
- Only 19% trust hallucination protection
- 40%+ of agentic AI projects expected cancelled by end 2027
